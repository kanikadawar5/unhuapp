About us

Unhu is the way an individual views the world around, with values of kindness, respect, consideration, humility towards others and self; and most of all a sense of responsibility towards people like us and different alike.

Being a social networking platform, Unhu-app, aims to make use of technology, using videos and content in order to assist people to never run out of interesting things to talk about in real life; encouraging harmony and interaction and bridging gaps between human relations.
